My Dearest Priya

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favorite

Our Journey

We met in July, in a group chat... That day, talking to you felt like finding something special. Your way of speaking, your warmth—it all drew me in. For 1-1.5 months, we talked so much. You asked, "What are we?" but I didn't have an answer because... I didn't realize my feelings yet.

When you stopped talking to me, I tried to forget you... but I couldn't. I was attached—your messages gave me peace, even if I told myself it was just affection. Then came Bangalore... the night you blocked me. I couldn't sleep, just thinking, Why? I couldn't even talk about it because I'd kept you a secret. Surrounded by people, I felt so alone.

After SIH, I wanted to share my happiness with you... but your replies hurt me. I decided—no more thoughts of you. The first week was hard, but slowly... I moved on. Or so I thought.

Then, on 19th February, your message lit up my night. My heart raced. You said I mattered in your life... and I let you back in. Since then, I'm lost in thoughts of you every day. It took me 7-8 months to realize... I love you so much, Priya. ❤️

"Priya,
I miss you more than I show.
I love you more than you know.

You're my peace, my happiness, my everything.
Will you be mine... forever?"
